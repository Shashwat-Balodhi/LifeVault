Butter Chicken Recipe — Mom's Special

Ingredients:
- 500g chicken thighs, boneless
- 1 cup yogurt
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 can (400ml) tomato puree
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 2 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- Salt to taste
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, turmeric, and salt for at least 2 hours.
2. Grill or pan-fry the chicken until charred. Set aside.
3. In a deep pan, melt butter and sauté onions until golden brown.
4. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add tomato puree, garam masala, and chili powder. Cook for 15 minutes on medium heat.
6. Add the cream and stir well. Simmer for 5 minutes.
7. Add the cooked chicken pieces. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan bread or basmati rice.

Tips:
- For extra richness, add a tablespoon of kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves).
- The sauce can be made a day ahead and tastes even better the next day.
- Adjust the chili powder to your spice preference.

Family recipe passed down from grandmother. Makes 4 servings.
